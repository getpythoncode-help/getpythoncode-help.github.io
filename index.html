NPTEL

The National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) was initiated by seven IITs (Bombay, Delhi, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Madras, Guwahati and Roorkee) along with the IIS Bangalore in 2003. It provides education to mainly these engineering branches civil engineering, computer science and engineering, electrical engineering, electronics and communication engineering and mechanical engineering and it has 235 courses in web/video format. It is a platform were you get genuine imfomation for preparing for any exam.
